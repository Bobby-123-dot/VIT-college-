Our History

The Vellore Institute was founded with a vision to bring world-class education to students in India and beyond. It started with a small group of students and faculty but quickly rose to national prominence.

  • Established in the year 1992 with a humble beginning.
  • Grew to become a multidisciplinary university within a decade.
  • Pioneered education for underprivileged and tribal students in Eastern India.
  • Now houses more than 30,000 students from over 60 countries.

Advisory Council

The Advisory Council of Vellore Institute brings together eminent personalities from academia, industry, and governance to guide strategic direction and academic excellence.

  • Includes former IAS officers, international educators, and global leaders.
  • Advises on global partnerships, research initiatives, and curriculum innovation.
  • Promotes a culture of leadership and social responsibility.
  • Ensures alignment with international standards and vision.

About the Founder

Prof. Achyuta Samanta, the visionary founder of Kalinga Institute, is a renowned educationist, social reformer, and philanthropist. His journey of transforming lives through education and empowerment has earned global recognition.

  • Founder of VIT and KISS, uplifting over 30,000 tribal children through education.
  • Recipient of numerous national and international awards including the Gandhi Peace Prize nomination.
  • Member of Parliament and advocate of inclusive growth and equal opportunities.
  • Continues to inspire millions with his dedication to nation-building and human welfare.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission criteria for VIT?
Admissions are based on entrance exams, merit lists, and counseling sessions. Eligibility varies by course; please check the official site for details.
Does VIT offer scholarships?
Yes, VIT offers merit-based and need-based scholarships to deserving students across various programs.
Are international students accepted?
Absolutely! VIT welcomes foreign students and provides special support services and cultural integration programs.
What kind of placement support is available?
VIT has a dedicated placement cell with strong ties to national and international recruiters, offering internships and full-time job placements.
